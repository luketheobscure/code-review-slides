Incentivize code review.

  • This is hard to do.
  • It's easy to celebrate shipping: demos are visible, review is invisible
  • It's easy to track AI usage metrics, PRs merged, tickets closed
  • "When a metric becomes a target, it ceases to be a good metric."
  • Engineers will map their effort to whatever incentive structures you build
  • If you want review done well: reward it
This is something that's easy for small companies and teams to do, but gets harder the bigger you get.
Engineers are logical, and will put effort where they see it rewarded.
