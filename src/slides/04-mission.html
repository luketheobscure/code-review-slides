Why we're here.

Our mission: "Help people help each other." When that's your mission, that creates a lot of internal pressure to deliver features quickly and deliver them safely. because I don't want a bug that I let slip to mean that someone doesn't get the help that they need. Or if I'm slow in delivering something like new sharing tools, and that means that a non profit isn't able to hit their goals
So we were early in looking at AI tools to see if they can help us move faster while still moving safely
