Fundamentals

I was thinking the other day about all of the technologies that at one point in my career I could say I was an expert in. Each of these logos up here is something that I spent way too much time learning. But that time wasn't wasted, even though we don't use these anymore. Because through learning these technologies, I learned fundamentals that I still use today. I learned about debugging, and testing. I learned about what the right level of abstraction is. What's too much boilerplate. I learned the fundamentals. And for some reason, with the hype around AI we've lost those fundamentals.
