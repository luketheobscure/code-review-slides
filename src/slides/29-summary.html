In Summary

● Learn from the past

  • Own your code. Understand your code.
  • Keep changes small.
  • Share responsibility.
  • Understand the requirements and the full system.
  • Incentivize code review.

● Look to the future

  • Use AI code review as a pre-pass, not a sign-off.
  • Triage every change by risk.
  • Invest in verification, not just review.
  • Review the whole system, not just the diff.
  • The ratio is shifting : 90% review is coming.
Here's the full picture. Five lessons from the past and five from the future. These aren't contradictory : they're complementary. The past lessons are about holding the line on what makes engineering work. The future lessons are about building the infrastructure for what's coming. You need both.
