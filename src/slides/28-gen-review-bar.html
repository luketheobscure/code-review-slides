Today we write code and review it. Tomorrow we may mostly review what agents write

Right now most teams are roughly split: engineers spend time writing code and time reviewing it. The ratio is starting to move. As agents get better, the generation side gets cheaper. The review side : which requires judgment, context, and accountability : stays expensive. We're not at 90% review yet. But the teams that will do best are the ones building that muscle now. Don't optimize to ship faster. Optimize to review better. The bottleneck is shifting from the keyboard to the eyeballs.
