Theory of Constraints

The throughput of any system is limited by its constraint.

Eliyahu M. Goldratt · The Goal · 1984

It makes sense that there's this new focus on the code review step if we think about the software development life cycle through the lens of the theory of constraints. The principle, from Goldratt's "The Goal": every system has one constraint, and throughput is set by it. 1. "maximizing every machine's efficiency actually **hurts** the system by creating pile-ups at the bottleneck." OR 2. "An hour lost at a bottleneck is an hour lost for the entire system. An hour saved at a non-bottleneck is a mirage."
